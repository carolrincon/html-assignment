About Me

I'm a 22 years old Colombian Filmmaker, but right now I'm an international student at Algonquin College, in Canada. I choose to study the IMD program to expand my field and contribute even more to my career expectations. I would like to make content for children, mostly for their learning process. From media content throughout videos, animations until book illustrations.

People says that I am a really courious person, because I'm into a lot of diverse activities. For instance, bookbinding, gardening, astronomy, bakering, learning japanese and of course Stop Motion!. If you are wandering if I have time for do all this... well just wait to know that I also opened the AC Stop Motion Club. We are just starting like a small community, but I have in mind many and fun plans to make it a pleasant club to be part and proud of.
